Love Is A Roguelite (LIAR)

TURN-BASED DECK-BUILDING ACTION GAME SET WITHIN A PROCEDURALLY GENERATED LOVE STORY

About

LIAR features a revolutionary LOVE SYNC system. This isn't your average AI - LOVE SYNC reacts to your every move, building a unique emotional connection with your companion throughout your adventure. The choices you make - in combat, conversations, and even the cards you play - all influence your companion's feelings. Will you win their heart with heroic deeds and thoughtful words, or will your actions spark a fiery (or icy!) romance? With LOVE SYNC, every playthrough feels fresh and unpredictable. Prepare to be surprised by the depth of your companion's emotions!

General Idea

LIAR is a game about love, challenges and building a relationship together.

SYNC Graph

The SYNC Graph is a UI element that showcases the current emotion of your companion. I was tasked with the visualization of emotion changes applying them to the graph and then updating the resulting values back to the companion character. The graph is composed of two Cartesian coordinate systems, one for the Excitement-Boredom and Joy-Sorrow axis of emotion and another rotated 45 degrees for the Trust-Fear and Serenity-Anger axis.
