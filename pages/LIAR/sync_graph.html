SYNC Graph

Grid drawing

The SYNC graph is used to represent the current emotion of our companion to the player. It is composed of various elipses representing the area where the emotion is active and smaller elipses to represent when an emotion is extreme

The graph works by having two Cartesian coordinate system, one to represent the Excitement-Boredom axis and Joy-Sorrow axis and the other, rotated 45 degrees showcases the Serenity-Anger and Trrust-Fear axis of emotions.

The graph uses vector math to be able to determine the ammount of each emotion that the companion has and transmit it back to the companion. Then using elipse calculations to determine whether or not the pip is inside the elipse of any emotion, it will update the graph to showcase the emotion is the most prominent.
