Siege Game

2D Strategy and base building tower defense game.

About

Siege game is a 2D base building game where you have to collect resources and use them to build defenses around yourself. Durin the day you will be free to explore and get resources, however during the night a horde of monsters will attack you and try to kill you. They can attack you with melee, long distance and worst of all: siege engines.

General Idea

The game was made by myself in 1 month using Unity. I utilized tilemapping for the scenary and online retrieved animations for characters
