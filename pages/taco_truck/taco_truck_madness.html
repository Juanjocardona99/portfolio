Taco Truck Madness

A fast paced and fun VR game made with unity.

About

Taco truck madness is a VR game where you have to cook tacos quickly to serve customers. A list of orders will tell you what to make and your remaining time. Your available ingredients are: Meat, onion, pineapple, cheese and sauce. Cook tortilas, protect your ingredients from rats and finish your orders quickly to stay in business!

Making Of

The project took 4 weeks to make, it was a collaboration between 6 teammates, we used Jira to manage the project and github for our version control.

Cuttable Component

My task within the project was to create a component that made the ingredients cuttable, making them separatable in up to 8 different pieces.

More

The cutting system of the game consists in two things: Cutter and cuttable Cutter is a component that emits a raycast and uppon detection of a cuttable object starts a cut. Cuttable works by having three different trigger zones that are generated automatically: two vertical ones for axis XY and ZY and an horizontal one for XZ. With this and using the distance between the initial point of contact and the last point of contact I can detect if the knife has cut. Then the model will be separated and the cuttable component will be added to the new halves so they are cuttable too.
